How to use:
You can export/ import your mindmap by clicking the down-/ upload arrows in the upper right corner.
Click on a node to open its context menu:
Either drag or click the plus-symbol to add a new node.
Long-press the edit-symbol to change the color of the node.
Click the arrow to (un)hide any child nodes.
Use the mouse-wheel to pan/ zoom.
